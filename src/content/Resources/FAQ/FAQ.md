# Frequently Asked Questions

## About Christianity

### Is the Bible corrupted?
No, the Bible is not corrupted. We have thousands of ancient manuscripts that confirm the reliability of the biblical text. Modern translations are based on these manuscripts and accurately reflect the original texts.

### Do Christians worship three gods?
No, Christians worship one God who exists in three persons: Father, Son (Jesus), and Holy Spirit. This is known as the Trinity. It's not three separate gods, but one God in three persons - a concept that reflects the complex nature of God.

### Why do you say Jesus is God?
Jesus claimed to be divine multiple times in the Gospels. He accepted worship, forgave sins (something only God can do), and referred to Himself with divine titles. His resurrection from the dead validates these claims.

## About Islam

### Is Mohammad a true prophet?
No. A true prophet of God must be consistent with previous revelation. Mohammad contradicted foundational biblical truths about God, Jesus, salvation, and more. Additionally, his personal life and actions do not reflect the character of someone speaking for the true God.

### Does the Quran predict Mohammad in the Bible?
No. Islamic claims that the Bible foretells Mohammad are based on misinterpretations of biblical texts. The passages cited by Muslims (like Deuteronomy 18:18) refer to Jesus or other biblical figures when read in context.

### Why do you criticize Islam so harshly?
We criticize false teachings, not individuals. We love Muslim people and want them to find salvation in Jesus Christ. Truth matters, especially when it concerns eternal salvation.

## About Salvation

### How can I be saved?
Salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ alone (Acts 4:12). You must acknowledge your sinful nature, believe that Jesus died for your sins and rose again, and confess Him as Lord of your life (Romans 10:9-10).

### Do good works contribute to salvation?
Good works are the result of salvation, not the cause of it. We are saved by grace through faith, not by works (Ephesians 2:8-9). Once saved, we naturally desire to do good works out of gratitude and love for God.

### Can Muslims be saved without leaving Islam?
True salvation requires acknowledging Jesus as Lord and Savior, which contradicts fundamental Islamic teachings. While Muslims can certainly come to faith in Christ, continuing to adhere to Islamic teachings about Jesus would be inconsistent with that faith.
