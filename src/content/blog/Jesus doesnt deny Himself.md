## Understanding the Context[](https://preview--truth-seeker-documents-site.lovable.app/biblical-truths/jesus-doesnt-deny-himself#understanding-context)

But because the man didn't know who Jesus was we see him asking Jesus what must HE do as in him believing he must do something instead of believing in what Jesus does.

This is also why the man walked away from Jesus when asked to sell his things. The man didn't actually love God but loved wealth which is why Jesus didn't reveal to that man that He was God because only the disciples who believed in God saw Jesus as God because their heart was pure as Jesus says blessed are the pure at heart for they shall see God.
