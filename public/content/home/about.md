# About Islam IsLIES

Islam IsLIES is dedicated to showing the truth about Islam and its false teachings through a Christian perspective.

Our mission is to help Muslims understand the true path to salvation through Jesus Christ.

We believe that salvation comes only through faith in Jesus Christ, not through works or following false prophets like Mohammad.

## Our Values

- **Truth**: We are committed to sharing the truth, even when it's difficult
- **Compassion**: We care deeply for Muslims who have been deceived
- **Biblical Focus**: All our teachings are grounded in the Bible
