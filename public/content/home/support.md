# Support Our Ministry

Islam IsLIES is a ministry dedicated to sharing the truth about Islam and helping Muslims find salvation through Jesus Christ. We rely on the support of believers who share our vision.

## How You Can Help

### Prayer

The most important way you can support us is through prayer. Please pray for:

- Muslims around the world to come to know Jesus
- Our team's safety and wisdom
- The effectiveness of our outreach
- Financial provision for this ministry

### Financial Support

Your financial gifts enable us to:

- Create and distribute resources
- Maintain our online presence
- Support missionaries working in Muslim-majority countries
- Translate materials into various languages

### Share Our Content

Help spread the truth by sharing our content on social media and with your friends and family.

Thank you for considering supporting our ministry. Your partnership makes a difference in bringing the truth of the Gospel to those who need it most.
